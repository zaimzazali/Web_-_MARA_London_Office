ABOUT THIS PROJECT
ALHAMDULILLAH we managed to establish one stop portal for MARA sponsored students in the United Kingdom and Ireland. The platform includes an online registration process, profile access and update, semester result submission and a communication channel between sponsored students and MARA London office.

In January 2019, a meeting was held to discuss potential digital move that was able to address issues on the processes towards an efficient workflow. Currently, the process involves a series of manual procedures , although it is simple, they are mainly tedious to begin with.  It requires students to complete forms manually, print and scan necessary documents as well as the back and forth emails for further enquiries and submission. Finally, to overcome those issue, we took on the project which aims to provide a seamless and integrated process to our students.

A special thanks to Mr Zaim Zazali, who had offered his experience in between his study to realise this effort. It is my hope that this portal will benefit MARA sponsored students for years to come!
